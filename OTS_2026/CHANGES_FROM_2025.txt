Changes from OTS_2025 to OTS_2026 (Pro Forma)
===============================================

This document records every constant changed from the 2025 to 2026
pro forma version. Values marked (est.) are estimated from inflation
adjustments; all others are from IRS Rev. Proc. 2025-32 or enacted
legislation (OBBB, CA SB 1144).

Scope reduction:
  - Removed all non-CA state solvers and form files
  - Removed formdata/ directory (no 2026 PDFs)
  - Version reset to 0.01 in all solvers
