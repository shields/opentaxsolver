DISCLAIMER - OTS_2026 Pro Forma Tax Year 2026
==============================================

This is an UNOFFICIAL, EXPERIMENTAL fork of OpenTaxSolver for pro forma
2026 tax year scenario modeling. It is:

  - NOT endorsed by, affiliated with, or supported by Aston Roberts or
    the OpenTaxSolver project.

  - NOT tax advice. Do not rely on this software for actual tax filing
    or compliance purposes.

  - NOT guaranteed to accurately reflect 2026 tax law or IRS/FTB rules.
    Many values are ESTIMATES based on inflation adjustments and
    preliminary legislation; official numbers may differ.

  - For scenario modeling and planning purposes ONLY.

  - USE AT YOUR OWN RISK.

This fork is based on known and estimated 2026 tax parameters as of
March 2026, including provisions from the "One, Big, Beautiful Bill"
(OBBB) and IRS Revenue Procedure 2025-32. No PDF form filling is
supported (no 2026 PDF forms exist). Only federal (US 1040) and
California (CA 540) returns are included.
